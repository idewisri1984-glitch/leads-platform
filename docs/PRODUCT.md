# Bali Leads Platform

Version: 1.0

Status: Product Definition

---

# Product Overview

Bali Leads Platform is an AI-powered B2B Lead Generation Platform.

Its purpose is to automate the entire process of finding new business opportunities anywhere in the world.

The platform combines search, artificial intelligence, CRM, and outreach into one integrated workflow.

---

# Target Users

The platform is designed for companies that sell products or services to other businesses.

Examples include:

- Furniture manufacturers
- Interior design studios
- Lighting manufacturers
- Hospitality suppliers
- Construction companies
- Architecture firms
- Textile suppliers
- Medical equipment suppliers
- Industrial manufacturers
- Software companies
- Consulting agencies

The platform is industry-independent.

# What the Platform Does NOT Do

The platform is not:

A generic CRM

An ERP

A website builder

A social media scheduler

A marketing automation platform

Its only purpose is AI-powered B2B Lead Generation.
